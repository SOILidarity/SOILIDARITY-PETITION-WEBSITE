A Land in Peril: The Problems We Face

Soil Degradation

Our foundation is crumbling! Aggressive quarrying and lingering lahar deposits are stripping away fertile topsoil, leaving our land barren, scarred, and vulnerable to erosion.

Ancestral Land Conflict

The rights of the Aeta community, the original stewards of this land, are being sidelined by commercial interests. This isn't just an environmental issue; it's a crisis of social justice.

Geohazard Risks

Living on unstable lahar is a ticking time bomb. With every rainy season, the risk of devastating landslides threatens homes, infrastructure, and, most importantly, human lives.

Understanding the Root Causes

Unsustainable Quarrying

Unregulated and irresponsible quarrying operations are carving up our landscape, a primary cause of the dust that chokes our air and the erosion that wounds our land.

Legacy of Pinatubo

Decades later, the eruption's massive lahar deposits still dominate the terrain. This has created a permanent environmental challenge demanding bold solutions.

Deforestation & Top-Down Planning

Widespread loss of trees destroyed nature's defenses. For too long, decisions have been made without consulting the people who live here, breeding distrust and failure.

Pioneering a Green Future: Our Solutions

Build Brilliantly!

Our "Lahar-to-Livelihood" program will empower farmers while creating new income streams.

👀

The People's Watchtower!

Form an INDEPENDENT monitoring team to ensure transparency and accountability.

🌳

Unleash the Green Shield!

Mandate "Green Buffer Zones" of native trees to fight pollution, crush erosion, and restore our ecosystem.
